★ Insights

The research powerhouse universities that form the Russell Group dominate research funding, being awarded the most amount projects and funding. However, they do not have significantly better funding success rate, with them being at the average of 20-30%. Inner city universities dominate, with the 'golden triangle' of research universities (London and Oxbridge) leading the way, but also Manchester, Glasgow and Birmingham universities recieveing considerably more than rural universities.
