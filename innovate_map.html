In 2021, Innovate UK awarded funding to over 3,000 projects, with a total value of £724 million. They allocated a large budget for supporting innovations in Manufacturing, Materials & Mobility, Disruptive technology and Ageing Society, Health & Nutrition. The most funded project is ONEheart by Airbus. It got 15 million pounds for supporting their innovations in efficiently air transport.
